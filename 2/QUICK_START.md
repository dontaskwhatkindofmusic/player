# Quick Start Guide

## Get Started in 30 Seconds

### Step 1: Open the Player
- Open `music-player.html` in your browser
- (Chrome, Safari, Edge, or Firefox)

### Step 2: Select Your Music Folder
- Click the green **"📁 Select"** button
- Choose a folder containing your music files
- Grant folder access when prompted

### Step 3: Start Playing
- Click any song in the library
- Or click the **"▶"** play button
- Use **Next/Previous** buttons to navigate

---

## Key Features at a Glance

| Feature | How to Use |
|---------|-----------|
| **Search** | Type in the search box (left sidebar) |
| **Shuffle** | Click the 🔀 button or toggle in Settings |
| **Repeat** | Click the 🔁 button or set mode in Settings |
| **Advanced Filters** | Go to **Filters** tab → click artist/genre/year chips |
| **Remove Songs** | Click the ✕ next to any song (hidden, not deleted) |
| **Volume** | Click the volume slider at the bottom |
| **Settings** | Click the ⚙️ button in the library header |

## Using with iTunes Library

1. Open music-player.html
2. Click **"📁 Select"**
3. Navigate to your Music folder:
   - **macOS**: `~/Music/Music/Media/Music`
   - **Windows**: `C:\Users\[YourName]\Music`
4. Select it and grant access
5. Done! All songs load automatically, including metadata

---

## Using on Your Phone

### iPhone (Safari)
1. Open `music-player.html` in Safari
2. Tap the **Share** button (↗️)
3. Select **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Tap **"Add"**
5. Open the app from your home screen
6. Select your phone's Files or iCloud Music folder

### Android (Chrome)
1. Open `music-player.html` in Chrome
2. Tap the **menu** (⋮) button
3. Tap **"Install app"**
4. Tap **"Install"**
5. Open the app from your home screen
6. Select your phone's Music folder or Downloads

---

## Lock Screen Controls

Once you start playing:
1. Press **Play/Pause** once
2. Lock your device
3. Control buttons appear on your lock screen
4. Use them to control playback without unlocking

---

## Keyboard Shortcuts

Press **Space** to toggle Play/Pause while the player is open.

---

## Settings You Should Know About

**Shuffle** — Randomly shuffles next song  
**Repeat** — Off / All songs / Single song  
**Volume** — Adjust playback volume  
**Removed Songs** — Clear songs you've hidden (doesn't delete files)  

All settings save automatically!

## Troubleshooting

### "No songs found"
→ Make sure your folder contains supported formats: MP3, WAV, M4A, FLAC, OGG, AAC

### "Select a folder to load your music"
→ Click the green 📁 button and choose a folder

### Metadata shows filename instead of artist/album
→ Your audio files might not have ID3 tags. Update them in iTunes or a tag editor.

### Not working on Firefox
→ Firefox doesn't support File System Access API yet. Use Chrome or Safari.
